Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner

Thoughtful, informed psychiatric care for individuals navigating challenging times

Who I Work With

I work with youth and adults who feel weighed down by challenges such as anxiety, depression, ADHD, sleep difficulties, self-esteem concerns, grief, or the emotional impact of cancer and perimenopause. Many people I see are functioning on the outside while feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or exhausted internally—often managing racing thoughts, low mood, poor sleep, or the strain of high responsibility.

I also support medical professionals, first responders, and business executives who are carrying significant stress while continuing to show up for others.

My Approach

My role is to offer a steady, non-judgmental space where we can slow things down and understand what is happening beneath the surface. I see medication as one possible tool—not a requirement, and never the whole answer.

We work collaboratively to clarify goals, explore options, and create a plan that aligns with your values, preferences, and lived experience. My focus is on helping you feel more like yourself again, at a pace that feels manageable and respectful.

Services

Psychiatric Evaluation

Comprehensive assessments to better understand symptoms, history, and contributing factors, with time and space to ask questions.

Medication Management

Thoughtful, collaborative medication support when appropriate, with attention to effectiveness, side effects, and individual goals.

Ongoing Follow-Ups

Continued care focused on stability, adjustment, and long-term wellbeing.
